This is a map of flashbulb memories associated with certain albums that are burned into my brain from a time of my life I would like to forget. After a dispute with my parents, I ended up graduating with a humanities undergradute degree before trying to move to New York with my friends. I sold my car to pay for rent, so when I had to move back home after not finding a job, I had no method of transportation outside of my sister's old hybrid bike from high school. The pinpoints indicate locations of specific memories I had the first few months I moved back when I was completely distraught leading up to the time I had saved enough money to buy a motorcycle. The memories are moments I strongly associated with what I was listening to for whatever reason, which helped me work through this difficult time.
