This is a map of flashbulb memories associated with certain albums that are burned into my brain from a certain time of my life. When my parents tried to get me to drop out of undergrad, I left with a humanities degree before trying to move to New York with some friends. I sold my car to pay for rent, so when I had to move back home after not finding a job, I had no method of transportation outside of my sister's old hybrid bike from high school. The pinpoints indicate locations of specific memories I had the first few months I moved back when I was leading up to the time I had saved enough money to buy a motorcycle. The memories are moments I strongly associated with what I was listening to for whatever reason.
